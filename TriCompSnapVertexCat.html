Triangle Vertex Manipulation Survey

Please read the following information carefully before proceeding.

Why we are doing this research: In this project, we aim to study how people understand the manipulation of a triangle's top corner location and angle size when given information about the other two bottom corners.
With this data we hope to learn more about the innate geometry intuitions we have and the mechanisms we use to employ them.

What will you have to do: You'll be shown the base of a triangle with its two angles. We will give you information on how these two angles will be changing and you must answer how this affects the top corner from a multiple choice bank.

Benefits and compensations: We do not expect any direct benefits to you from your taking part in this research.
You will receive a compensation in the amount indicated in Amazon Mechanical Turk task description upon completion of the survey.

Potential risks: There are no potential risks to this study.
You are free to leave at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.

Privacy: Your responses will be kept anonymous and stored indefinitely on our password-protected server.
The study does not collect any direct or indirect individual identifiers throughout, with the exception of the Amazon Mechanical Turk ID.
Please mark your Amazon profile as private.
Your Amazon Mechanical Turk ID is kept on a different file from the data-set and will be deleted upon payment.

Duration: Approximately 45-60 minutes. There is no time limit for completion except for the mTurk HIT limit.

Experiment Instructions:

Welcome!
This study examines how people judge shape changes in triangles. Specifically, how might changes to the distance between the bottom two corners of a triangle or to their angle sizes affect the third corner?

Procedure:

First, read the description indicating a change to the bottom two corners of the triangle that is displayed on the screen. Then, select the multiple choice response that accurately reflects what happens to the third corner as a result. Please try to be accurate as possible and answer the questions to the best of your ability. When you're done with each question, please press the "Continue‚Äù button to move to the next question.
The experiment takes between 45-60 minutes. At the end of the experiment you'll be provided with a user ID code that you should enter back in mTURK.

Please don't press on the refresh or backwards buttons, since the experiment will begin anew, and your results will not be saved.

Press "Start" when you're ready to go.

Good luck!

Explanation Page

This page illustrates what we mean by increasing/decreasing the distance between the bottom two corners of the triangle or increasing/decreasing the angle size of the bottom two corners.
Please press and hold each button on the right to view how the distances or the angle sizes change given each type of verbal instruction. Each change is made with reference to the initial triangle shown, so you can toggle between the change and the original triangle by pressing the button multiple times. You will see a variety of initial triangles from which to imagine a change during the course of the experiment.
